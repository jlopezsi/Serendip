if you like paul verhoeven's later work robocop total recall black book you should take the time to delve into his dutch language work br br this is a serious work showing class differences in 19th century holland and the total lack of concern for workers the title character takes a slew of meaningless jobs after the family is forced to move to the city eventually ending up as a prostitute to survive br br hearing the typical streets paved with gold dreams that were typical of america at that time we can totally relate to those driven from their farms br br women were certainly toys for men even doctors to play with and rape if they chose br br it was interesting to see rutgar hauer in a role as a gentlemen and the experience of monique van de ven was not to be missed
